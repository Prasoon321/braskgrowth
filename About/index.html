    ABOUT BRASK GROWTH

    OUR STORY

    Brask Growth was born from a deep appreciation for the timeless beauty of Kanjivaram silk sarees and the cherished memories they hold. Inspired by the sarees worn by our grandmothers and mothers—pieces that still shine with the same elegance today—we wanted to carry forward this legacy for future generations. Every Amavi saree tells a story of tradition, family, and craftsmanship, woven together with passion and skill that has been passed down through centuries.

    Our Mission

    Our mission at Brask Growth is to create heirloom-quality sarees that honor centuries-old craftsmanship while catering to the modern woman's sense of style. We strive to empower the skilled artisans who weave these sarees by providing them with sustainable livelihoods, ensuring their art thrives for years to come. By blending tradition with contemporary aesthetics, we aim to offer sarees that not only adorn but also connect wearers to a deeper cultural and personal legacy.

    Our Vision

    At Brask Growth , our vision is to preserve and promote the rich heritage of Kanjivaram silk sarees, ensuring that future generations continue to appreciate and cherish this timeless artistry. We envision a world where handwoven silk sarees are celebrated not just for their beauty but also for the traditions, craftsmanship, and stories they carry. Our goal is to bridge the gap between generations, bringing this cultural legacy into the contemporary fashion landscape, making every Brask Growth saree a symbol of elegance, heritage, and lasting beauty.

    The Company

    At Brask Growth, every saree is more than just a garment—it is a labor of love, crafted with meticulous attention to detail. We are committed to using only the finest silk and the most skilled artisans, ensuring that each saree is of the highest quality. From the intricate patterns of brocade to the delicate pastels, every Brask Growth saree is a celebration of heritage and artistry, designed to be cherished for a lifetime.
