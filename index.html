    ABOUT BRASK GROWTH

    OUR STORY

    Brask Growth was born from a deep appreciation for the timeless beauty of Kanjivaram silk sarees and the cherished memories they hold. Inspired by the sarees worn by our grandmothers and mothers—pieces that still shine with the same elegance today—we wanted to carry forward this legacy for future generations. Every Amavi saree tells a story of tradition, family, and craftsmanship, woven together with passion and skill that has been passed down through centuries.
